Yantai Hill

A landmark hill of a coastal city.

Overview

Best Time to VisitDaytime
ActivitiesSightseeing
Crowd LevelNot Crowd

Yantai Mountain is Yantai's landmark. The mountain, only 43 meters high, features a signature lighthouse. There are consulates of countries like the UK and the US with distinctive architectural styles. The stone carvings in the scenic area are renowned. Visitors can climb up for a panoramic view of Yantai's coastline.

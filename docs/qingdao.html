Qingdao

Qingdao is located along the coast of the Yellow Sea and is renowned for its beautiful coastline and pleasant climate. It is also beloved by tourists for its unique German-style architecture. Qingdao Beer is a well-known beer brand, and the annual Qingdao International Beer Festival held every summer attracts a large number of domestic and international visitors.

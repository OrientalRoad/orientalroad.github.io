China Nationwide - Top

China varies greatly in landscape, climate, and culture. The Northeast features forests and snowy winters; the Northwest has deserts and grasslands. The Southwest includes plateaus and canyons, while the Southeast coast offers beautiful seaside views. Northern regions experience four distinct seasons, contrasting with the warm, humid South. The Qinghai-Tibet Plateau stands as the "Roof of the World." Han Chinese dominate, alongside numerous ethnic minorities with unique traditions.

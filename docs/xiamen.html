Xiamen

Xiamen boasts unique natural coastal scenery. The coastline of Xiamen Island is picturesque, making it an excellent place for cycling and walking. Gulangyu Island, known as the "Island of Music," features many European-style buildings and attracts numerous visitors with its natural environment and tranquil lifestyle.

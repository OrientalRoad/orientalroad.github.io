Jingdezhen

Jingdezhen, often referred to as the "Porcelain Capital" of China, has been a hub for ceramic production for over 1,700 years. The city's rich history and unparalleled craftsmanship in porcelain have earned it global recognition. Visitors can delve into the world of ceramics through visits to ancient kilns, museums, and artisan workshops, making it a must-see destination for Chinese Cultural Heritage enthusiasts.

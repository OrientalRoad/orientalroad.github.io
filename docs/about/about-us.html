About Us ​

Our team has been dedicated to promoting travel to Chongqing for people around the globe since 2010. We are delighted to see Chongqing gradually become popular online. With China's visa policies becoming increasingly clear, we decided to launch this website in 2024 to promote the magical Eastern country to more China travel enthusiasts.

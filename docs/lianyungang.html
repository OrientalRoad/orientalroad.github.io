Intro

Lianyungang is located in the northeastern part of Jiangsu Province, bordering the Yellow Sea to the east, the Huabei Plain to the west, Yancheng and Huai'an to the south, and Rizhao, Shandong Province to the north.

NameLianyungang
ClimateTemperate monsoon climate
Area7,615 km²
Population4 million
GeographyEast China, west coast of Haizhou Bay

Introduction

Located in the old town of Guangzhou, Hualin Temple is an ancient temple that blends religious belief with artistic beauty. It not only preserves exquisite Buddhist sculptures and murals but also houses a wealth of historical relics, making it an important site for studying Lingnan Buddhist culture. A stroll through the temple offers a deep sense of Zen tranquility and showcases the unique charm of traditional Chinese architecture and craftsmanship.

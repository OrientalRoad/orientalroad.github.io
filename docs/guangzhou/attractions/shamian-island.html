Shamian Island
Best Time to VisitDaytime
ActivitiesSightseeing

Shamian Island is a sparkling gem on the Pearl River in Guangzhou, featuring architectural treasures that blend Chinese and Western cultures. Walking along its stone-paved streets, you'll find historic buildings with exotic charm on both sides, transporting you back to the concession era of a century ago. Today, the island has become a hub for art, cafes, and creative spaces, offering visitors a relaxing retreat.

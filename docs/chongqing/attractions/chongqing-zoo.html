The zoo was opened to the public in January 1955. It covers an area of 43.5 hectares. Chongqing Zoo currently has more than 20 giant pandas, making it one of the zoos with the largest number of giant pandas in the country.

How to get there

Navigate to Chongqing Zoo

Upon exiting the light rail, you will arrive directly at the zoo’s main entrance. From there, it’s just a 3-minute walk (200 meters) to see the giant pandas.

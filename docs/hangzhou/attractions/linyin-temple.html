Introduction

Lingyin Temple was founded in the first year of the Xianhe era during the Eastern Jin Dynasty (326 AD), boasting a long history of nearly 1700 years. Within the Chinese Buddhist community, Lingyin Temple enjoys an extremely high reputation.

Suzhou

Suzhou, located on the eastern shore of Tai Lake, is an ancient city with a long history, renowned for its classical gardens, picturesque water towns, and exquisite cultural arts.

NameSuzhou, Soochow
ClimateSubtropical monsoon maritime climate
Area8,657.32 km²
Population12 million
GeographySoutheastern Jiangsu Province, on the eastern shore of Tai Lake

Seasons

Best time to visit

Peak Season
Mar – May, Sep – Nov
Spring brings mild weather and blooming flowers, especially in Suzhou's gardens, which are breathtaking. Autumn is perfect for admiring maple leaves, with the red foliage complementing the ancient architecture beautifully.
Off-season
Jun – Aug, Dec – Feb
Less visitors and lower prices.

Laojun Cave, located in the Nan'an District of Chongqing, is a hidden Taoist sanctuary amidst the city. As one of the most important Taoist temples in the Chongqing area, it attracts countless visitors with its long history, beautiful natural scenery, and mysterious religious atmosphere. Explore the ancient town of Ciqikou â€‹

Watch from 9:54

Ciqikou Ancient Town is one of Chongqing's most famous cultural landmarks, with a history spanning over a thousand years. It features well-preserved architectural clusters from the Ming and Qing dynasties, where stone-paved streets, stilt houses, and ancient docks transport visitors back in time. As one of the birthplaces of Ba-Yu culture, Ciqikou attracts countless visitors with its rich historical heritage and unique folk customs. Whether you're savoring authentic Chongqing street food or shopping for exquisite handicrafts, this place offers a deep dive into the local culture.

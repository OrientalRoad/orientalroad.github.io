Chongqing Travel: 3-Night, 5-Day Weekend Adventure with Japanese Friends

Gathering YouTube awesomeness...
Over the weekend, a group of Japanese friends embarked on a 3-night, 5-day trip to Chongqing. They explored the city's modern architecture and historical temples, indulged in local cuisine and culture, and learned about its rich history. As one of China's four municipalities, Chongqing boasts a population of over 30 million, making it one of the largest cities in the world. The video highlights Chongqing's historical significance, including its role as the wartime capital during World War II and the bombings by the Japanese army. The trip concluded with a delightful taste of local delicacies and breathtaking views of Chongqing's night skyline.
Destination: Chongqing
Serving as an economic center in southwestern China, Chongqing is known for its unique mountainous terrain and modern urban development, earning it the nickname "magical city." Videos showcasing its attractions have gone viral on TikTok, making it a popular tourist destination in recent years. Additionally, its cuisine is renowned worldwide, with hot pot being the most well-known dish.

Must-Try Experience: Chongqing Xiao Mian ​

Watch from 8:37

Chongqing Xiao Mian is one of the representatives of Chongqing's local culture, loved for its spicy, numbing, fresh, and fragrant flavors. This noodle dish is typically handmade and served with specially prepared chili, Sichuan peppercorns, peanut sauce, and other seasonings, creating a unique taste experience. Whether in small street-side shops or upscale restaurants, this classic Chongqing delicacy can be enjoyed. For first-time visitors, we recommend choosing the "mild spicy" option to gradually adapt to its distinctive spiciness.

Exploring a Thousand-Year-Old Temple Amidst City Skyscrapers ​

Watch from 10:09

Lohan Temple is located in the heart of Chongqing, amidst a forest of skyscrapers, and stands as a historically significant and highly revered Buddhist temple with nearly a thousand years of history. As one of the most famous ancient temples in Chongqing, it has witnessed the city's transformation while attracting countless believers and tourists with its unique location and profound cultural heritage. Built along the contours of a hill, the temple offers a serene retreat despite being surrounded by the bustling urban landscape. Inside, 500 lifelike statues of Arhats are enshrined, showcasing the exceptional craftsmanship of ancient artisans. With its vibrant incense offerings, Lohan Temple is an ideal place for blessings, meditation, and experiencing the depth of Buddhist culture.

Attraction: Luohan Temple

Exploring Hongyadong: The Internet's Hottest Attraction ​

Watch from 13:10

Hongyadong is one of the most iconic landmarks in Chongqing, located along the Jialing River, where traditional Ba-Yu architecture blends seamlessly with modern urban scenery. It is renowned for its unique stilt houses, creating an impressive vertical "sky town." At night, Hongyadong lights up brilliantly, reflecting off the river and offering one of the best views of Chongqing's stunning nighttime landscape. Visitors can wander through the area, soaking in its rich historical and cultural atmosphere while savoring authentic Chongqing cuisine, all while experiencing the unique charm of this mountain city.

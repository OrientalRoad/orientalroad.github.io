Kunming

Kunming, the capital of Yunnan Province, is often referred to as the "Spring City" due to its mild climate and year-round spring-like weather. Known for its beautiful natural scenery, rich ethnic diversity, and vibrant cultural heritage, Kunming offers visitors a unique blend of modern development and traditional charm. Whether you're exploring ancient temples, enjoying scenic lakes, or discovering the local ethnic markets, Kunming is a must-visit destination in southwestern China.
